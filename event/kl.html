3RD Self Employability Training Program

organised by Multimedia Development Corporation (MDeC)

Date: August 29, 2014

Venue: Makmal Bestari, Faculty of Education Section 17, UiTM Shah Alam

The program is opened to all TESL students who are interested to leverage on existing language skills to earn additional income. During the session, training assessment was given to participants on English Transcriptions and translation tasks.
