ASIAN MANAGEMENT CONFERENCE & EXHIBITION 2014

Organised by Malaysian Institute of Management

Date: October 13, 2014 & October 14, 2014

Venue: The Royale Chulan Hotel, Kuala Lumpur

The Asian Management Conference & Exhibition is a bi-annual event designed to facilitate sharing of resources, knowledge and regional information in support of today's interconnected management world where the demand for ready access to up-to-date management information, thinking and views are imperative to succeed in this changing region.
