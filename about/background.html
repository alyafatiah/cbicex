  • Crowdsourcing is one of the Digital Malaysia initiatives to focus specifically on two key drivers: innovation and creativity, and productivity. Under Digital Malaysia, crowdsourcing industry has been identified as a potential industry to increase the income of low-income earners.

  • The industry provides employment and income to individuals who are interested. ICT-oriented programs are formed in order to complete the National Transformation Agenda that will drive the economy towards a high-income nation by 2020. Multimedia Development Corporation (MDeC) has been mandated as the lead agency for the Digital Malaysia initiative.
