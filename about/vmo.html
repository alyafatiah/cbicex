Vision, Mission & Objective

  1. Home
  2. Vision, Mission & Objective

Our Mission

To be a leading centre in producing researchers and experts to develop technopreneurship solutions and business solution for crowdsourcing.

Our Vision

To enhance the image of the university by producing excellent researchers and be a referral centre for ICT and business processes for crowdsourcing solutions.

Our Objective

There are 5 objectives of CBIC :

  • To serve as a research centre for crowdsourcing business area.
  • To integrate expertise from ICT industries and other related fields for the advancement and innovation of research in crowdsourcing.
  • To provide support in the government implementation program and initiatives that related to crowdsourcing.
  • To establish national and international linkages to enhance the quality and innovative research.
  • To serve as one-stop centre and reference body for crowdsourcing business ecosystem.
