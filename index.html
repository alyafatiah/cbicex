About Us

Realizing on the potential of crowdsourcing as a new entrepreneurial evolution in digital economy that offers various research areas to be explored, in 2012, Faculty of Computer and Mathematical Sciences (FSKM) has taken this opportunity to set up the Crowd Business Innovation Centre (CBIC).

  Our Mission






To be a leading centre in producing researchers and experts to develop technopreneurship solutions and business solution for crowdsourcing.

   Our Vision






To enhance the image of the university by producing excellent researchers and be a referral centre for ICT and business processes for crowdsourcing solutions.

  Our Objective






To serve as a centre for research, consultation, sharing information and training. It will become a centre of innovation in the production of high-impact ideas related to crowdsourcing.

Scope & Function

Research


To conduct technopreneurship research that focus on crowdsourcing workers platforms and job providers in the area of services application network system and social research.

Consultation Service



To provide consultation and advice that is related to the development of crowdsourcing business industries.



Industry Linkage



To establish linkages with stakeholders in national international crowdsourcing industries and supporting them in terms of network creation and program developments.

Training



To design prepare and update training modules as well as monitoring the quality of trainings.
